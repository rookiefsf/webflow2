your Magic Game

IPad Product

Magic players now have a more graphic solution to deck listing and statistics than for example TappedOut. Utilizing gradients as this graphic first impression, these elements from the game itself communicate more quickly to users which deck they are editing not only in button elements but screen treatment when expanded. The real backbone to this product however is the Strategy Data Visualization which is also crucial to the second piece to this product: the PlayMat.

PlayMat Product

What also makes this approach unique is the additional products associated with its use. Once a deck has been documented this data would generate a printed file that players can order. This not only can be a revenue source, but another great CX product that is designed for beginning to medium level players who want an edge when trying to learn/teach new strategies.
